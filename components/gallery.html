Welcome,

This is a space for you to get ideas of where to travel next (and maybe more importantly, where to eat). I love researching new places before I visit and reading through lots of reviews from different sites; so here are my collective findings all in one place. I am based in Birmingham, UK so many of the recommendations are of Birmingham and other cities in England, but as I travel and discover new countries and cities around the world, I will document it here. Throughout the posts I have included little tips which I have highlighted in green and any accommodation/food/ activities that I would recommend are highlighted in pink. I have also included hyperlinks to other websites that could be of interest. Circular_Motion is for people to find some inspiration about their next adventure, whether that be a coffee in the cotswolds or a solo adventure to Morocco. I hope you enjoy browsing the archives and pick up a few ideas along the way.

Nina x
